# Research Prompt: Pay-As-You-Go Sanctions Screening Market Pricing

## Objective
Research the current market pricing for pay-as-you-go / per-search sanctions screening services. I need to understand how competitors price their screening APIs and platforms to benchmark our pricing strategy of $3.00 USD per search.

## What I Need

### 1. Direct Competitors — API-based Sanctions Screening
Find pricing for services that offer per-search or per-call sanctions screening, including:
- ComplyAdvantage
- Refinitiv World-Check (LSEG)
- Dow Jones Risk & Compliance
- Sanctions.io
- NameScan
- Comply.Dog
- Sanction Scanner
- KYC Hub
- Alessa (formerly CaseWare RCM)
- Ondato
- Sumsub (sanctions screening module)
- Persona (watchlist screening)
- Trulioo
- Veriff (AML screening)
- Any other pay-as-you-go sanctions screening providers

### 2. Pricing Models to Capture
For each provider, find:
- **Per-search / per-call pricing** (pay-as-you-go rate)
- **Volume tier pricing** (e.g., 1K, 10K, 100K searches/month)
- **Minimum monthly commitment** (if any)
- **What's included per search**: single list check vs. all lists, ongoing monitoring, match details, risk scoring
- **Setup fees or platform fees**
- **Free trial / free tier availability**

### 3. Scope Differences
Note what each provider includes in a single "search":
- Number of sanctions lists checked (OFAC only? Global? PEP? Adverse media?)
- Real-time vs. batch screening
- Match resolution (automated vs. manual review)
- Ongoing monitoring (included or extra cost?)
- Adverse media screening (included or extra cost?)
- Enhanced due diligence / OSINT (included or extra cost?)
- API access vs. UI-only

### 4. Market Segmentation
- What do **SMB / startup** focused providers charge? (e.g., Sanctions.io, NameScan)
- What do **enterprise** providers charge? (e.g., Refinitiv, Dow Jones)
- What do **fintech-embedded** providers charge? (e.g., ComplyAdvantage, Sumsub)

### 5. Comparison Framework
Present findings in a table format:

| Provider | Per Search Cost | Lists Covered | PEP Included | Adverse Media | Ongoing Monitoring | EDD/OSINT | Min Commitment |
|----------|----------------|---------------|--------------|---------------|-------------------|-----------|----------------|

### 6. Key Questions to Answer
- What is the **market average** per-search cost for basic sanctions screening?
- What is the **market average** for sanctions + PEP + adverse media combined?
- How does **$3.00 per search** compare to market rates for a service that includes:
  - Automated L1 sanctions screening (2.1M+ entities)
  - Automated L2 OSINT / enhanced due diligence
  - Automated adverse media search
  - PEP screening
  - AI-generated compliance report
  - Deputy MLRO risk assessment
- Is there a gap in the market for **fully automated end-to-end screening with EDD** at a fixed per-search rate?
- What do providers charge separately for **enhanced due diligence** reports? (typically $50-500+ per case when done manually)

## Output Format
Provide a structured report with:
1. Summary table of all providers and pricing
2. Market average pricing by tier (basic screening / screening + PEP / full EDD)
3. Competitive positioning analysis for a $3.00 per-search all-inclusive service
4. Recommendations on pricing strategy
